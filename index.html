Frameless for iOS 8 is the browser that gets out of your way. It's made for designers and developers who need an easy way to preview prototypes on iOS devices.

Download for free Source on Github
Transparent UI

No browser chrome and no status bar. Gestures control browser history and reveal the keyboard.

Customizable gestures

Swipe, tap with three fingers or shake the device. If anything gets in your way, just disable it.

Built for prototyping

Works great with Framer Studio mirroring or previewing any HTML prototypes in a "native" context.

Made for designgineers. But works for anyone who wants a simple,
full-screen web browsing experience.
